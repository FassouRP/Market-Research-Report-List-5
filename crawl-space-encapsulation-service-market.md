<p><p>Crawl space encapsulation service market can be divided into two main types: plastic-based and concrete-based. Plastic-based encapsulation involves installing a durable plastic liner over the crawl space floor and walls to create a moisture barrier. This method is commonly used in homes with moisture issues. Concrete-based encapsulation, on the other hand, involves sealing the crawl space with a layer of concrete to prevent moisture and pests from entering. This method is often preferred for its durability and longevity.</p></p>
