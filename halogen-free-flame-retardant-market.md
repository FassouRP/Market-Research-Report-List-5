<p><p>Halogen Free Flame Retardant market types include Aluminum Hydroxide, Magnesium Hydroxide, Nitrogen, Zinc, and Others. Aluminum Hydroxide works as a flame retardant by releasing water vapor to dilute flammable gases. Magnesium Hydroxide acts as a flame retardant by releasing water vapor and absorbing heat. Nitrogen-based flame retardants prevent combustion by diluting oxygen. Zinc-based flame retardants function by forming a protective layer on the material surface. Other types of flame retardants include intumescent systems, phosphorus-based compounds, and inorganic fillers.</p></p>
