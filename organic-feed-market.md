<p><p>Organic feed market is classified into two main types: powder and particle. Powder organic feed is finely ground and easily digestible by animals such as poultry and small livestock. It is suitable for young animals or those with sensitive digestive systems. Particle organic feed consists of coarser grains and is commonly used for larger animals like cattle and horses. Both types of organic feed contain natural, non-GMO ingredients and are free from synthetic additives, making them a healthier choice for animals and the environment.</p></p>
