<p><p>The foreign exchange market consists of various types of transactions including currency swaps, outright forwards, FX swaps, and FX options. Currency swaps involve the exchange of principal and interest payments in different currencies. Outright forwards are agreements to exchange a specific amount of currency at a future date at an agreed-upon exchange rate. FX swaps involve the simultaneous purchase and sale of a currency for two different value dates. FX options give the holder the right, but not the obligation, to exchange currencies at a specified rate on a future date.</p></p>
