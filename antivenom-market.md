<p><p>Antivenom, also known as antivenin, is a medication used to treat venomous bites or stings. The animals produce antibodies to the venom, which are then collected and purified to create antivenom.</p><p>The Antivenom Market is experiencing steady growth, with a projected CAGR of 7.6% during the forecast period. <p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Antivenom Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyvalent antivenom</li><li>Monovalent antivenom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyvalent antivenom targets multiple types of venom from various species of snakes, scorpions, spiders, and other venomous animals. It offers a broad spectrum of protection against different venoms, making it a versatile option for treating bites or stings from multiple sources. On the other hand, monovalent antivenom is specific to one type of venom, offering targeted protection against bites or stings from a single species. While polyvalent antivenom is more widely used, monovalent antivenom may be preferred in cases where the specific venom is known.</p></p>
