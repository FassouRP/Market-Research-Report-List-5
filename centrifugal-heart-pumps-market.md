<p><p>Centrifugal heart pumps market is divided into two main types: implantable heart pump devices and extracorporeal heart pump devices. Implantable heart pump devices are surgically implanted inside the body to help the heart pump blood more efficiently. Extracorporeal heart pump devices, on the other hand, are external devices that temporarily support heart function outside of the body, often during surgical procedures or while waiting for a heart transplant. Both types play a crucial role in treating various heart conditions and improving patients' quality of life.</p></p>
