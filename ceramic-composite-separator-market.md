<p><ul><li>Polyolefin Separator</li><li>Polyester Non-Woven Separator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic composite separators have two main types in the market - polyolefin separators and polyester non-woven separators. Polyolefin separators are made of a type of thermoplastic polymer that offers high chemical resistance and stability, making them suitable for various applications. On the other hand, polyester non-woven separators are composed of polyester fibers that are bonded together to form a porous structure, providing excellent resistance to heat and mechanical stresses. Both types of separators are widely used in batteries and other energy storage applications.</p></p>
