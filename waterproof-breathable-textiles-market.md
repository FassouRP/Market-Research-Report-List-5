<p><p>Waterproof breathable textiles can be categorized into three main types - densely woven, coated, and membrane. Densely woven textiles are tightly stitched fabrics that repel water on the surface. Coated textiles have a waterproof layer applied to the outer surface, preventing water absorption. Membrane textiles have a waterproof and breathable layer sandwiched between fabric layers, allowing moisture to escape while keeping water out. Each type has its own benefits and applications in various industries such as outdoor gear, sportswear, and medical garments.</p></p>
