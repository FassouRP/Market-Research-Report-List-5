<p><p>Satellite communication antenna systems are classified based on the frequency band they operate in, with Ka-band and Ku-band being two common types. Ka-band antenna systems operate in the frequency range of 26.5-40 GHz, offering high data transfer rates and capability for small dish sizes. On the other hand, Ku-band antenna systems operate in the frequency range of 12-18 GHz, providing reliable and cost-effective communication solutions. Both systems are widely used in various applications such as broadcasting, telecommunications, and military communications.</p></p>
