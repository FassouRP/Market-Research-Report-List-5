<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic</li><li>Electro-Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Batch control systems can be categorized into two main types - electromagnetic and electro-pneumatic. Electromagnetic batch control systems utilize electrical currents to control the flow of materials in a batch process. On the other hand, electro-pneumatic batch control systems use a combination of electricity and compressed air to control the batching process. Both types of systems offer precise and efficient control over the handling of materials in batch processes, catering to different industry requirements and preferences.</p></p>
