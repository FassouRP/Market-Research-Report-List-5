<p><p>Small road rollers are essential construction equipment used for compacting surfaces such as roads and pavements. There are two main types in the market: single drum road roller and double drum road roller. Single drum road rollers have a large drum at the front for compaction, making them suitable for larger projects. Double drum road rollers have drums at the front and back, providing more uniform compaction, making them ideal for smaller or tighter spaces. Both types offer efficient and effective compaction for a variety of construction projects.</p></p>
