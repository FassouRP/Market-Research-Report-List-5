There are two main market types for QAMS: On-premise and Cloud-based. On-premise QAMS is installed and maintained on the organization's own servers, providing full control and security but requiring higher initial costs. Cloud-based QAMS is hosted on the vendor's servers, offering lower upfront costs, scalability, and ease of access from anywhere with an internet connection. Both market types cater to different needs and preferences of organizations.</p></p>
