<p><p>PVA water soluble film market is divided into two main types: cold water-soluble films and hot water-soluble films. Cold water-soluble films dissolve quickly in cold water, making them ideal for applications where low temperatures are required. Hot water-soluble films, on the other hand, dissolve rapidly in hot water, making them suitable for applications where high temperatures are needed. Both types of films offer a convenient and environmentally friendly solution for packaging, as they dissolve completely without leaving any residue.</p></p>
