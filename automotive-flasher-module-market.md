<p><p>Automotive flasher modules are essential components in vehicles that control the blinking of turn signals, hazard lights, and other indicators. There are two main types of flasher modules in the market: Electronic Flasher Modules and Thermal Flasher Modules. Electronic flasher modules use electronic circuitry to control the flashing of lights, offering more reliability and customization options. On the other hand, Thermal flasher modules use a bimetallic strip that heats up and cools down to control the flashing, offering a simpler yet less versatile solution.</p></p>
