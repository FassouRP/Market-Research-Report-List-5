<p><p>Digital drum kits are available in three main market types based on the material of the drum heads: rubber heads, mylar heads, and mesh or silicone heads. Rubber heads provide a softer feel and quieter sound, making them ideal for practice at home. Mylar heads offer a more realistic drumming experience and are commonly used in entry-level electronic drum kits. Mesh or silicone heads are the most advanced option, providing a highly realistic feel and response akin to acoustic drums, making them popular among professional drummers.</p></p>
