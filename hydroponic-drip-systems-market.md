<p><p>Hydroponic Drip Systems market can be divided into two main types: Passive and Active systems. Passive hydroponic drip systems rely on gravity to deliver nutrient solution to plants through a network of tubing and drippers. They are simpler and less expensive to set up. Active hydroponic drip systems, on the other hand, utilize a pump to deliver the nutrient solution, offering more control over the delivery rate and ensuring consistent distribution. Both systems are popular choices for hydroponic growers seeking efficient and effective ways to nourish their plants.</p></p>
