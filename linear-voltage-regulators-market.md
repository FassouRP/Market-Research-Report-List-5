<p><p>Linear voltage regulators are a type of electronic component used to control and stabilize the output voltage of a power supply. There are two main types of linear voltage regulators in the market: Standard and Low Dropout (LDO). Standard linear voltage regulators offer fixed output voltage and are widely used in various applications. LDO regulators, on the other hand, provide a more precise output voltage and have a smaller dropout voltage, making them suitable for low-power and portable devices. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring a stable and reliable power supply for electronic devices.</p></p>
