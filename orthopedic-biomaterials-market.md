<p><p>Orthopedic biomaterials market is categorized into two main types: Metal orthopedic biomaterials and Non-metal orthopedic biomaterials. Metal orthopedic biomaterials are commonly used for orthopedic implants due to their strength and durability, such as titanium and stainless steel. Non-metal orthopedic biomaterials include polymers, ceramics, and composites, which are utilized for their biocompatibility and ability to mimic the properties of human tissues. Both types of biomaterials play a crucial role in orthopedic surgeries and treatments, catering to the diverse needs of patients and healthcare providers.</p></p>
