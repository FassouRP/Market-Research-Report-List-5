<p><ul><li>Woven Geotextile</li><li>Nonwoven Geotextile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Geotextile products are divided into two main types: woven geotextiles and nonwoven geotextiles. Woven geotextiles are manufactured by weaving together polypropylene or polyester threads, providing strength and durability for applications such as road stabilization and erosion control. Nonwoven geotextiles are made by bonding fibers together through chemical or thermal processes, offering filtration and drainage properties for projects like landscaping and drainage systems. Both types play a vital role in civil engineering and construction projects for soil stabilization and reinforcement.</p></p>
