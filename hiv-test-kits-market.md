<p><strong>The HIV Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antibody Tests</li><li>RNA (viral load) Test</li><li>Antibody-antigen Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HIV test kits come in different types to detect the presence of HIV in the body. Antibody tests detect antibodies produced by the immune system in response to HIV infection. RNA (viral load) tests detect the genetic material of the virus itself, measuring the amount of virus in the blood. Antibody-antigen tests detect both antibodies and antigens, which are proteins on the surface of the virus. Each type of test serves a specific purpose in diagnosing HIV infection.</p></p>
