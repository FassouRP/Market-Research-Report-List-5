<p><p>Agricultural microbials can be categorized into different types based on their composition and function. Bacterial microbials consist of beneficial bacteria that promote plant growth and protect against harmful pathogens. Fungal microbials include beneficial fungi that can enhance nutrient uptake and improve soil health. Composite microbials are a combination of different microbial species that work together to boost plant productivity. Other types of agricultural microbials may include biopesticides, biostimulants, and biofertilizers that offer various benefits to crops and soil health.</p></p>
