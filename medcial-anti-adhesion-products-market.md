<p><p>Medical anti-adhesion products are designed to prevent abnormal tissue attachments that can occur during surgeries or healing processes. There are two main types of anti-adhesion products in the market: anti-adhesion films and anti-adhesion gels. Anti-adhesion films are thin sheets that are placed between tissues to prevent them from sticking together. Anti-adhesion gels, on the other hand, are applied directly to the tissues and form a barrier to prevent adhesions. Both types of products help to reduce the risk of complications and improve patient outcomes in various medical procedures.</p></p>
