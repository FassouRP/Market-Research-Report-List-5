Common types of thermoplastics used in micro molding include Polyether ether ketone (PEEK), Polyvinyl chloride (PVC), and Polyethylene (PE). PEEK is known for its high strength and temperature resistance, PVC is widely used for its durability and flexibility, while PE is popular for its lightweight and chemical resistance properties. These materials play a crucial role in various industries for producing precision components.</p></p>
