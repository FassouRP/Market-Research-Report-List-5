<p><p>Specialty resistors market consists of two main types - SMD Type and Through Hole Type. SMD Type resistors are surface-mount components that are soldered onto the surface of a circuit board, offering compact design and high performance. On the other hand, Through Hole Type resistors are mounted through holes in the circuit board, providing secure mechanical support and ease of assembly and repair. Both types cater to different market segments based on their unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
