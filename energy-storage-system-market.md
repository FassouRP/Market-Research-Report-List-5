<p><p>The energy storage system market consists of three main types: electrochemical energy storage, thermal storage, and mechanical energy storage. Electrochemical energy storage involves the conversion of electrical energy into chemical energy for storage and later use. Thermal storage utilizes heat energy to store and release energy as needed. Mechanical energy storage involves storing energy in mechanical forms such as compressed air or flywheels. Each type of energy storage system has its own advantages and applications in various industries for efficient and sustainable energy management.</p></p>
