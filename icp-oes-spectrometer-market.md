<p><p>ICP-OES Spectrometer market offers two main types of systems - Sequential Type and Simultaneous Type. Sequential Type ICP-OES operates by measuring elements one at a time, providing accurate results but requiring more time for analysis. On the other hand, Simultaneous Type ICP-OES can measure multiple elements simultaneously, allowing for faster analysis and increased efficiency. Both types are widely used in various industries such as environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, and food and beverage testing to determine the elemental composition of samples with precision.</p></p>
