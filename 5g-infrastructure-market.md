<p><p>The 5G infrastructure market comprises four main types of cells: femtocell, pico cell, micro cell, and macro cell. Femtocells are small, in-home devices that provide coverage for a few meters. Pico cells cover a larger area, such as a small building or outdoor space. Micro cells cover a few hundred meters and are commonly used in urban areas. Macro cells cover several kilometers and are used to provide coverage in larger geographic areas. Each type of cell plays a role in creating a seamless 5G network infrastructure.</p></p>
