<p><p>Wall Mounted Ozone Disinfection Machines are available in two main types: Glass Medium and Non-Glass Dielectric Medium. Glass Medium machines use glass tubes to generate ozone through a corona discharge process, while Non-Glass Dielectric Medium machines use non-glass dielectric material. Glass Medium machines are known for their high efficiency in ozone production, while Non-Glass Dielectric Medium machines are preferred for their durability and longer lifespan. Both types are commonly used for disinfection purposes in various settings.</p></p>
