<p><p>Industrial servo amplifiers play a crucial role in various industrial applications. The market is categorized into two main types: Single Axis and Multi-Axis servo amplifiers. Single Axis servo amplifiers control the movement of one axis or motor, while Multi-Axis servo amplifiers are capable of controlling multiple axes simultaneously. Single Axis amplifiers are suitable for applications with simpler movements, while Multi-Axis amplifiers are ideal for complex machinery requiring coordinated motion control of multiple axes. Both types offer precise and efficient control of industrial machines and equipment.</p></p>
