<p><p>Well test separators are used in the oil and gas industry to separate and measure the production from wells. The market is categorized into three types based on the phases being separated - TwoPhaseTestSeparator, ThreePhaseTestSeparator, and FourPhaseTestSeparator. Two-phase separators separate gas and liquid, three-phase separators separate gas, oil, and water, while four-phase separators separate gas, oil, water, and solids. Each type of separator addresses specific production requirements and variations in the fluid composition from different wells.</p></p>
