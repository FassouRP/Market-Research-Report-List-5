<p><p>Copper foil for lithium-ion batteries can be categorized into two main types: electrolytic copper foil and rolled copper foil. Electrolytic copper foil is produced through an electroplating process, resulting in a smooth and shiny surface, making it ideal for high-performance lithium-ion batteries. On the other hand, rolled copper foil is manufactured by rolling copper sheets to achieve a thinner and more flexible material, suitable for applications requiring a high degree of flexibility and conductivity. Both types play a crucial role in the performance and efficiency of lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
