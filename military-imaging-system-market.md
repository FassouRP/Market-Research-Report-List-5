<p><p>Military Imaging System market has various types including Thermal Imaging, Hyperspectral Imaging, and Multispectral Imaging. Thermal Imaging systems detect heat signatures to create images in low-light or nighttime conditions. Hyperspectral Imaging systems capture a wide range of spectral bands for detailed analysis of materials and objects. Multispectral Imaging systems combine several narrow spectral bands for enhanced detection and classification capabilities. These technologies play a crucial role in providing intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance capabilities for military applications.</p></p>
