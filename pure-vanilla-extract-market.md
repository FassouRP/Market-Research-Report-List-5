<p><p>Pure Vanilla Extract comes in two main market types: liquid and powder. Liquid vanilla extract is the most common form, made by soaking vanilla beans in alcohol and water. It is versatile and easy to use in a variety of recipes. Powdered vanilla extract, on the other hand, is made by drying and grinding vanilla beans into a fine powder. It provides a more concentrated flavor and is often preferred for certain applications where liquid extract may not be suitable. Both forms offer the classic, rich taste of pure vanilla.</p></p>
