<p><p>UV Flexo inks are commonly categorized into two main types based on their composition: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based UV Flexo inks are environmentally friendly and easier to clean up, making them a popular choice for sustainable packaging solutions. On the other hand, solvent-based UV Flexo inks offer faster drying times and are more resistant to fading or smudging. Both types of UV Flexo inks are widely used in the packaging industry for their versatility and ability to produce high-quality prints.</p></p>
