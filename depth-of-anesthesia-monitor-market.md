<p><p>Depth of Anesthesia Monitor Market encompasses various types such as BIS (Bispectral Index), Narcotrend, and E-Entropy, among others. These monitors help anesthesiologists assess a patient's depth of anesthesia during surgery, reducing the risk of awareness and ensuring optimal sedation levels. BIS monitors use a numerical scale to measure brain activity, while Narcotrend provides a detailed analysis of the patient's EEG waveform. E-Entropy monitors use a combination of EEG and EMG signals to determine the patient's level of consciousness.</p></p>
