<p><strong>The XRF Analysers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wavelength Dispersive (WDXRF)</li><li>Energy Dispersive (EDXRF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>XRF Analysers Market consists of two main types: Wavelength Dispersive (WDXRF) and Energy Dispersive (EDXRF). WDXRF analyzers use diffraction crystals to separate X-ray wavelengths, providing precise elemental analysis. On the other hand, EDXRF analyzers utilize semiconductor detectors and software algorithms to measure X-ray energy levels, offering faster analysis with lower detection limits. Both types are widely used in industries such as mining, environmental monitoring, and pharmaceuticals for their accuracy, sensitivity, and efficiency in elemental analysis.</p></p>
