<p><p>The X-Ray Tube Holder market can be categorized into two main types: Floor-Standing and Suspended. Floor-Standing X-Ray Tube Holders are mounted on the floor, providing stability and support for the X-Ray tube. They are commonly used in large medical facilities where mobility is not a concern. On the other hand, Suspended X-Ray Tube Holders are attached to the ceiling, allowing greater flexibility and mobility for the X-Ray tube. They are frequently utilized in smaller medical settings where space is limited and maneuverability is essential.</p></p>
