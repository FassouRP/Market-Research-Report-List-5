<p><p>Heavy duty encoders are utilized in industries requiring robust and long-lasting position feedback systems. There are two main types in the market - Incremental Encoder and Absolute Encoder. Incremental encoders generate pulses in response to incremental shaft movement, providing relative position information. On the other hand, Absolute encoders provide precise position information by assigning a unique code to each position. Both types cater to different needs in various industries, offering solutions for accurate and reliable position feedback systems.</p></p>
