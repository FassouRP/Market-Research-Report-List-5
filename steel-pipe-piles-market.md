<p><p>Steel pipe piles are commonly used in construction projects for their durability and strength. The market offers various types of steel pipe piles, including Spiral Weld Pipe, Electric Resistance Weld, and Double Submerged Arc Weld. Spiral Weld Pipe is formed by spirally winding steel strips and welding them together. Electric Resistance Weld piles are created by passing an electric current through the steel strips to form a weld. Double Submerged Arc Weld piles involve welding steel plates together while submerged in a flux to protect the weld.</p></p>
