<p><strong>The Fire Fighting Pre-action Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trench Fire Fighting Pre-action Systems</li><li>Flange Fire Fighting Pre-action Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Trench Fire Fighting Pre-action Systems are designed to protect areas where water damage needs to be minimized, such as data centers and museums. These systems consist of pipes laid underground with strategically placed nozzles. Flange Fire Fighting Pre-action Systems, on the other hand, are typically used in industrial settings where flammable liquids are present. These systems have special flanges that release water only when the fire detection system is activated, preventing accidental discharge. Both types of systems are crucial in preventing fire damage and protecting valuable assets.</p></p>
