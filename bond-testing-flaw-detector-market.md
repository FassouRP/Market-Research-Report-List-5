<p><p>The Bond Testing Flaw Detector market can be segmented into three types based on the testing methods used: Ultrasonic Testing, Eddy-current Testing, and Others. Ultrasonic Testing involves sending high-frequency sound waves through a material to detect internal flaws. Eddy-current Testing relies on inducing electrical currents in the material and measuring the response to identify defects. The Others category includes alternative methods such as radiographic testing and electromagnetic testing. Each type of testing offers unique capabilities for detecting flaws in bonds and materials.</p></p>
