<p><p>Flexible fabric sensors come in various types including flexible capacitive sensors, flexible piezoresistive sensors, flexible piezo sensors, flexible inductive sensors, and flexible fiber optic sensors. Each of these sensors has unique characteristics and uses in different applications. Flexible capacitive sensors can measure deformation and pressure, flexible piezoresistive sensors can detect changes in resistance due to pressure, flexible piezo sensors can convert mechanical energy into electrical signals, flexible inductive sensors use electromagnetic induction for sensing, and flexible fiber optic sensors utilize light propagation for measurement.</p></p>
