<p><p>Veterinary coagulation analyzers are classified into two types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic analyzers require minimal human intervention as they perform all tasks automatically, from sample loading to result reporting. Semi-automatic analyzers, on the other hand, require some manual steps such as sample loading and result interpretation. Both types of analyzers are important in veterinary medicine for analyzing blood coagulation parameters, detecting clotting disorders, and monitoring anticoagulant therapy in animals.</p></p>
