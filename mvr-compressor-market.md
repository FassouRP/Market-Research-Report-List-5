<p><strong>The MVR Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Type</li><li>Roots Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MVR compressors are commonly categorized into two main types in the market - centrifugal type and Roots type. </p><p>Centrifugal type MVR compressors utilize centrifugal force to increase the pressure and flow of the gas. They are known for their high efficiency and are commonly used in applications requiring high volumes of gas.</p><p>On the other hand, Roots type MVR compressors operate by trapping and transporting gas in between a pair of meshing rotors. They are popular in applications requiring a constant and pulsation-free flow of gas.</p></p>
