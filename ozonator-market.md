<p><p>Ozonator market is divided into three main types based on the technology used: UV Irradiation Ozonator, High Intensity Discharge Ozonator, and Electrolytic Type Ozonator. UV Irradiation Ozonators use ultraviolet light to produce ozone, while High Intensity Discharge Ozonators utilize electrical discharges. Electrolytic Type Ozonators create ozone through the process of electrolysis. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, making them suitable for different applications such as water treatment, air purification, and disinfection.</p></p>
