<p><ul><li>Delivery Conveyor</li><li>Injection Conveyor</li><li>Queue Conveyor</li><li>Extended Queue Conveyor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Baggage conveyor systems are essential for efficient operations in airports and other transportation hubs. The four main types of baggage conveyor systems are Delivery Conveyor, Injection Conveyor, Queue Conveyor, and Extended Queue Conveyor. </p><p>Delivery conveyors move baggage from the check-in area to the sorting area. Injection conveyors inject baggage into the main conveyor system. Queue conveyors manage the flow of baggage in a queue before loading onto the plane. Extended queue conveyors handle overflow baggage during peak times. Each type plays a vital role in ensuring smooth and timely baggage handling processes.</p></p>
