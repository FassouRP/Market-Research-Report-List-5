Differential pressure instruments measure level by detecting pressure differences, guided wave radar uses electromagnetic waves, non-contact radar measures level without touching the substance, ultrasonic instruments use sound waves, capacitance instruments detect changes in capacitance, and other types may utilize different technologies for accurate level measurement.</p></p>
