<p><p>Wave energy devices market can be categorized into different types. Point absorbers capture energy from small waves, oscillating water columns convert wave energy into air pressure, oscillating body converters use the motion of floating bodies to generate power, and overtopping wave power devices use the water that flows over a structure to generate energy. Additionally, there are other devices such as terminator devices, floating attenuators, and wave attenuators that also play a role in harnessing wave energy for power generation.</p></p>
