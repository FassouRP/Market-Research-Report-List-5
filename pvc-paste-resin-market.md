<p><p>PVC Paste Resin is produced using different methods, with two main types being the Micro Suspension Method and Emulsion Method. In the Micro Suspension Method, the resin is prepared by dispersing PVC particles in a liquid medium, resulting in a high quality product suitable for applications requiring excellent thermal stability. On the other hand, the Emulsion Method involves polymerizing vinyl chloride in an aqueous emulsion, leading to a resin with superior mechanical properties and good dispersion in water-based systems. Both methods have their own advantages and are used in various industries.</p></p>
