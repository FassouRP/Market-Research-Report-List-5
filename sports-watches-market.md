<p><p>Sports watches market can be categorized into two main types based on the technology used: Mechanical watches and Electronic watches. Mechanical watches are powered by a spring mechanism and require winding to keep accurate time. They are known for their craftsmanship and reliability. On the other hand, electronic watches are powered by batteries and use quartz crystals to keep time accurately. They are popular for their precision and advanced features such as heart rate monitoring and GPS tracking. Both types cater to different consumer preferences in the sports watch market.</p></p>
