<p><p>Buccal tissue matrix is a biocompatible scaffold used in oral surgery procedures to support tissue regeneration. There are three main types of buccal tissue matrix on the market: collagen, synthetic, and PTFE. Collagen matrices are derived from natural sources and closely mimic the extracellular matrix in the body. Synthetic matrices are made from artificial materials, providing a customizable option for specific patient needs. PTFE matrices are non-absorbable and offer long-lasting support for tissue repair. Each type of matrix has its own advantages and applications in oral surgery.</p></p>
