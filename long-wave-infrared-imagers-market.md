<p><p>Long-Wave Infrared Imagers are used for detecting thermal radiation. There are two main types of such imagers in the market - Unrefrigerated and Refrigerated. Unrefrigerated Long-Wave Infrared Imagers are cost-effective and suitable for applications where cooling is not necessary.
