<p><p>Automatic endoscope cleaners are categorized into two main types: single chamber cleaners and multi chamber cleaners. Single chamber cleaners are designed to clean one endoscope at a time, making them ideal for smaller facilities with lower volume of endoscopes. On the other hand, multi chamber cleaners are equipped with multiple chambers, allowing them to clean several endoscopes simultaneously, making them suitable for larger facilities with higher volume of endoscopes. Both types of cleaners offer efficient and automated cleaning of endoscopes, ensuring proper disinfection and compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
