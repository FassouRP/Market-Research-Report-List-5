<p><p>Advanced ceramic additives play a crucial role in enhancing the properties of ceramics by acting as dispersants and binders. Dispersants are additives that help in achieving a uniform distribution of ceramic particles in a liquid medium, resulting in improved strength and durability of the final product. On the other hand, binders are substances that hold the ceramic particles together, providing cohesion and stability to the material. Both dispersants and binders are essential components in the production of high-quality advanced ceramics.</p></p>
