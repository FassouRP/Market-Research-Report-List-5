<p><p>Community Antenna Television (CATV) Amplifiers are used to boost the signal strength of cable TV networks. There are two main types of CATV amplifiers - Outdoor CATV Amplifiers and Indoor CATV Amplifiers. Outdoor CATV amplifiers are typically installed outside on utility poles or overhead lines to amplify the signal before it reaches individual homes. On the other hand, Indoor CATV amplifiers are installed inside buildings to boost the signal strength within a specific area or building. Both types of amplifiers play a crucial role in ensuring clear and reliable cable TV reception for viewers.</p></p>
