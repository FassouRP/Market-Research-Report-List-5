<p><p>Kitchen filters market can be divided into two main types: Duct filters and Non-duct filters. Duct filters are connected to the kitchen exhaust system to remove grease, smoke, and odors before releasing air outside. Non-duct filters, on the other hand, are standalone filters that recirculate air back into the kitchen after removing impurities. Both types of filters play a crucial role in maintaining a clean and healthy kitchen environment by capturing pollutants and improving air quality.</p></p>
