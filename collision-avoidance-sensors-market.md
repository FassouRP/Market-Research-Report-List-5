<p><p>Collision avoidance sensors market is divided into four main types: Radar, Camera, Ultrasound, and LiDAR. Radar sensors use radio waves to detect vehicles or obstacles around a vehicle. Camera sensors use image processing to identify objects in the path of a vehicle. Ultrasound sensors use sound waves to determine the distance between the vehicle and obstacles. LiDAR sensors use lasers to create a 3D map of the surroundings. These sensors play a crucial role in preventing accidents and improving overall road safety.</p></p>
