<p><p>The Ceramic Auxiliaries market consists of various types including Rheology Modifier, Defoamer, Dispersant, Wetting Agent, and Others. Rheology modifiers are used to control the flow behavior of ceramic suspensions, while defoamers help in reducing foam formation during processing. Dispersants aid in preventing agglomeration of particles, and wetting agents improve the wetting and dispersion of materials. Other types of auxiliaries may include binders, plasticizers, or colorants, which play specific roles in ceramic processing and applications.</p></p>
