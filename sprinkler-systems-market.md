<p><p>Sprinkler systems are fire protection systems that use water to extinguish or control fires in buildings. Sprinkler systems help prevent the spread of fires and save lives by quickly extinguishing flames.</p><p>The global sprinkler systems market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.2% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to increasing awareness about fire safety and stringent regulations mandating the installation of sprinkler systems in residential and commercial buildings. Furthermore, the development of eco-friendly and energy-efficient sprinkler systems is also gaining traction in the market. These systems use less water and energy, making them more sustainable and cost-effective solutions for fire protection.</p></p>
