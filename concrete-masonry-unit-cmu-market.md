<p><p>Concrete Masonry Units (CMUs) are commonly divided into two main market types: Load Bearing Block and Non-Load Bearing Block. Load Bearing Blocks are designed to support the weight of a structure and are commonly used in exterior walls, structural columns, and foundations. Non-Load Bearing Blocks, on the other hand, are used to partition interior spaces and provide aesthetic finishes. Both types offer a durable and cost-effective solution for construction projects, with load-bearing blocks ensuring structural integrity and non-load-bearing blocks adding versatility and design options.</p></p>
