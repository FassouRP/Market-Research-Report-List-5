<p><p>Magnet rings are classified into two main market types: radial and axial. Radial magnet rings have magnetic poles arranged in a circular pattern around the ring, providing a strong and uniform magnetic field. They are commonly used in electric motors and generators. On the other hand, axial magnet rings have magnetic poles arranged along the axis of the ring, making them suitable for applications requiring a linear magnetic field, such as in sensors and actuators. Overall, both radial and axial magnet rings cater to different market needs based on the direction of the magnetic field required.</p></p>
