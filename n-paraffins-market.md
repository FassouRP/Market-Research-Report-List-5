<p><p>n-Paraffins are hydrocarbons that can be classified into two main types based on their molecular weight and boiling point. Light type n-Paraffins have lower molecular weight and boiling point, making them ideal for use in products like solvents, lubricants, and aerosol propellants. Heavy type n-Paraffins have higher molecular weight and boiling point, making them suitable for applications such as fuels, waxes, and lubricating oils. Both types of n-Paraffins are essential in various industries due to their distinct properties and versatility.</p></p>
