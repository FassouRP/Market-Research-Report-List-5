<p><p>Green manure is a type of cover crop used to improve soil fertility and structure. Leguminous green manure, such as clover and alfalfa, fix nitrogen in the soil through a symbiotic relationship with bacteria. Non-leguminous green manure, like rye and oats, provide organic matter to improve soil structure and reduce erosion. Both types of green manure are important in sustainable agriculture practices to increase crop yields and reduce the need for chemical fertilizers.</p></p>
