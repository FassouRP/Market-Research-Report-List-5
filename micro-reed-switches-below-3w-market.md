<p><p>Micro Reed Switches (Below 3W) market includes two main types: Normally Open Type and Normally Close Type. Normally Open Type reed switches are normally off, but when a magnetic field is applied, the switch closes or turns on. Normally Close Type reed switches are the opposite, where the switch is normally on but opens when a magnetic field is detected. These switches are widely used in applications where low-power switching is required, such as in sensors, security systems, and automotive electronics.</p></p>
