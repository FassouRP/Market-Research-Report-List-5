<p><p>Digital monitoring cameras can be classified into two main types: Infrared and non-infrared. Infrared digital surveillance cameras use infrared technology to capture clear images in low-light or nighttime conditions. These cameras are ideal for outdoor or low-light environments. On the other hand, non-infrared digital surveillance cameras rely on traditional lighting sources to capture high-quality images. They are suitable for well-lit indoor areas where infrared technology is not necessary. Both types of cameras play a crucial role in ensuring security and monitoring activities effectively.</p></p>
