<p><strong>The Voyage Data Recorder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>General Voyage Data Recorder</li><li>Simplified Voyage Data Recorder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>General Voyage Data Recorders (VDRs) are sophisticated devices used on ships to record crucial data for investigation in case of accidents or incidents at sea. They capture audio from the bridge, radar images, and key navigational data. Simplified Voyage Data Recorders (S-VDRs), on the other hand, are a more basic version that focuses on essential data like vessel position, speed, and heading. S-VDRs are usually mandatory for smaller vessels that do not require the comprehensive data recording capabilities of a full VDR.</p></p>
