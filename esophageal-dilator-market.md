<p><p>Esophageal dilators are medical devices used to widen the esophagus in cases of narrowing or strictures. There are two main types of esophageal dilators: mechanical and balloon dilators. Mechanical dilators are typically tube-shaped instruments that are manually inserted and expanded to widen the esophagus. Balloon dilators, on the other hand, use a balloon inflated with liquid or air to dilate the esophagus. Both types of dilators are important tools in the treatment of esophageal conditions such as strictures and dysphagia.</p></p>
