<p><p>Particle size analyzers are instruments used to determine the size distribution of particles in a sample. Two main types of particle size analyzers are nano particle size analyzers and micron particle size analyzers. Nano particle size analyzers measure particles in the nanometer range, typically below 100 nm, while micron particle size analyzers measure particles in the micrometer range, typically between 1 to 1000 micrometers. These analyzers are used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and materials science for quality control and research purposes.</p></p>
