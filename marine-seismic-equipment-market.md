<p><strong>The Marine Seismic Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sub-Bottom Profilers</li><li>Seismic Sensors</li><li>Scalar Sensor</li><li>Vector Sensor</li><li>Geophones & Hydrophones</li><li>Streamers</li><li>Single Streamer</li><li>Multiple Streamers</li><li>Air / Water Guns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine seismic equipment market types include sub-bottom profilers, seismic sensors (including scalar and vector sensors), geophones, hydrophones, streamers (single and multiple), and air/water guns. Sub-bottom profilers are used to image below the seafloor, while seismic sensors detect seismic waves. Scalar sensors measure pressure changes, while vector sensors measure both pressure and particle motion. Geophones and hydrophones detect ground vibrations and underwater sound waves, respectively. Streamers are towed behind vessels to record seismic data. Air/water guns produce acoustic signals for seismic surveys.</p></p>
