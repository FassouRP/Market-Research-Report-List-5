<p><p>Dental surgical loupes are magnification tools used by dentists and surgeons to enhance visibility during procedures. There are two main types of dental surgical loupes: clip-on loupes and headband-mounted loupes. Clip-on loupes attach directly to eyeglasses or safety goggles, providing a portable and customizable magnification solution. On the other hand, headband-mounted loupes are secured to a headband that is worn around the head, offering a hands-free magnification option for longer procedures. Both types of loupes play a crucial role in improving precision and accuracy during dental surgeries.</p></p>
