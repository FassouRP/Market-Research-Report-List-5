<p><p>Automotive HVAC blower motors are classified into two main types - brushed and brushless. Brushed blower motors use brushes to conduct current, making them simple and cost-effective but less efficient and prone to wear over time. On the other hand, brushless blower motors do not have brushes, resulting in improved efficiency, longevity, and reduced maintenance requirements. These motors are popular in modern vehicles due to their advanced technology and performance benefits. Both types cater to different customer needs and preferences in the automotive HVAC market.</p></p>
