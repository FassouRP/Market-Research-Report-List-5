<p><ul><li>Starch-based</li><li>Starch Blend with PLA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Degradable mulch films are categorized into two main types - starch-based and starch blend with PLA. Starch-based mulch films are made primarily from natural starch sources and easily break down into organic matter. Starch blend with PLA mulch films combines starch with polylactic acid to create a more environmentally-friendly option that still offers durability and degradability. Both types offer a sustainable alternative to traditional plastic mulch films, reducing plastic waste and promoting eco-friendly agricultural practices.</p></p>
