<p><p>Silicon steel sheets are widely used in the electrical industry due to their magnetic properties. There are two main types of silicon steel sheets on the market: Oriented Electrical Steel and Non-Grain Oriented Electrical Steel. Oriented Electrical Steel is processed in such a way that its magnetic properties are aligned in a specific direction, making it ideal for transformers and motors. Non-Grain Oriented Electrical Steel, on the other hand, has random magnetic properties, making it suitable for applications like electric motors and generators.</p></p>
