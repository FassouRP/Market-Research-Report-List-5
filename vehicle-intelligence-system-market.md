<p><p>Vehicle Intelligence System market can be categorized into three main types: RTS System, RSD System, and NVS System. RTS System refers to Real-Time Traffic Management System, which uses real-time data to optimize traffic flow and reduce congestion. RSD System stands for Road Safety Detection System, which uses sensors and cameras to detect potential hazards on the road and alert drivers. NVS System, or Navigation and Vehicular System, provides drivers with advanced navigation tools and information for enhanced driving experience. These systems play a key role in enhancing safety and efficiency on the road.</p></p>
