<p><p>Media gateways in the analog market convert voice signals from traditional analog devices to digital signals for transmission over IP networks. These gateways are used in legacy telephone systems to connect traditional landline phones with modern VoIP systems. On the other hand, digital media gateways handle digital voice signals directly, facilitating seamless communication between different digital systems and networks. Both analog and digital media gateways play a crucial role in enabling interoperability and communication between various devices and technologies in today's interconnected world.</p></p>
