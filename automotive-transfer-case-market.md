<p><strong>The Automotive Transfer Case Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gear Driven Transfer Cases</li><li>Chain Driven Transfer Cases</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gear Driven Transfer Cases use gears to transfer power from the transmission to the front and rear axles of a vehicle. These transfer cases are known for their durability and ability to handle heavy loads. On the other hand, Chain Driven Transfer Cases utilize chains instead of gears to transfer power. They are often lighter and more compact than gear driven transfer cases, making them popular in smaller vehicles or those focused on fuel efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in distributing power and torque to all four wheels of a vehicle for optimal performance.</p></p>
