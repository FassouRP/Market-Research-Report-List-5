<p><p>Solar charge controllers are essential for managing the power output of solar panels to batteries. There are two main types of solar charge controllers in the market - Pulse-Width Modulation (PWM) charge controller and Maximum Power Point Tracking (MPPT) charge controller. PWM controllers regulate the voltage of the panels to match the battery voltage, while MPPT controllers maximize the power output from the panels by constantly adjusting the voltage and current to ensure the panels operate at their peak efficiency.</p></p>
