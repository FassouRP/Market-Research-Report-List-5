<p><p>Sintering paste is used in various industries for joining and bonding components through a sintering process. Two main types of sintering processes are pressure sintering and pressure-less sintering. In pressure sintering, the paste is subjected to high pressure during the sintering process, while in pressure-less sintering, no external pressure is applied. Each type offers different advantages and is used in specific applications based on the requirements of the components being bonded.</p></p>
