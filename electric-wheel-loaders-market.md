<p><p>Electric wheel loaders are classified into two main market types: Electric Hybrid and Pure Electric. Electric Hybrid wheel loaders combine an electric motor with a diesel engine, offering improved efficiency and reduced emissions compared to traditional diesel-powered machines. On the other hand, Pure Electric wheel loaders run solely on electric power, eliminating emissions entirely. Both types are gaining popularity due to increasing environmental concerns and regulations, as well as the growing availability of renewable energy sources to power electric equipment.</p></p>
