<p><ul><li>HPCCL</li><li>HSCCL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Frequency High Speed CCL market types include High Performance CCL (HPCCL) and High Speed CCL (HSCCL). HPCCL is designed for applications requiring high reliability and performance, such as aerospace and defense industries. It offers low dielectric loss, high thermal conductivity, and high mechanical strength. On the other hand, HSCCL is suitable for high-speed digital and RF applications, with characteristics like low insertion loss, low dispersion, and controlled impedance. Both types cater to different needs in the high-frequency, high-speed electronic circuit market.</p></p>
