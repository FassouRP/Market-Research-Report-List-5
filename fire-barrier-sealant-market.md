<p><strong>The Fire Barrier Sealant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastometric Type</li><li>Intumescent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire barrier sealants are classified into two main types: Elastometric type and Intumescent type. Elastometric sealants are flexible and can accommodate movement in the building structure, making them ideal for joints and penetrations. On the other hand, Intumescent sealants expand when exposed to heat, forming a charred layer that insulates the substrate from fire. Both types of sealants are essential for maintaining fire safety in buildings by preventing the spread of fire through gaps and openings.</p></p>
