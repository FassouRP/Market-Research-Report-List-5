<p><p>Video servers market types include M-JPEG, MPEG-4, and H.264 technologies, each offering unique advantages. M-JPEG technology delivers high-quality video but requires more bandwidth. MPEG-4 technology provides efficient compression with good video quality. H.264 technology offers high-quality video with low bandwidth usage, making it ideal for streaming applications. These technologies cater to different needs in the market, ranging from professional surveillance systems to online video streaming services. Each technology has its own strengths and is chosen based on specific requirements of the user.</p></p>
