<p><p>Anti bedsore mattresses can be classified into two main types: static type and dynamic type. </p><p>Static type mattresses are designed to provide constant pressure redistribution by evenly distributing the weight of the patient. These mattresses are suitable for patients at low risk of developing bedsores.</p><p>Dynamic type mattresses, on the other hand, are equipped with adjustable air chambers that constantly change pressure points to prevent bedsores. These mattresses are ideal for patients at a higher risk of developing bedsores and require more advanced pressure relief.</p></p>
