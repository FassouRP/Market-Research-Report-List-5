<p><ul><li>Shaking</li><li>Reverse-air cleaning</li><li>Pulse-jet bag cleaning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bag dust collectors are commonly classified into three types based on the cleaning mechanism: shaking, reverse-air cleaning, and pulse-jet bag cleaning. In shaking bag dust collectors, the bags are cleaned by mechanically shaking them to dislodge the accumulated dust. Reverse-air cleaning involves using a reverse flow of air to clean the bags. Pulse-jet bag cleaning utilizes bursts of compressed air to dislodge the dust from the bags. Each type offers different advantages and is suitable for different applications based on factors such as dust load and particle characteristics.</p></p>
