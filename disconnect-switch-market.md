<p><p>Disconnect switches are devices used to isolate electrical equipment for maintenance or shutdown purposes. There are two main types of disconnect switches in the market: Fused disconnect switches and Non-fused disconnect switches. </p><p>Fused disconnect switches have a fuse as an additional safety measure to protect against overcurrent and short circuits. Non-fused disconnect switches do not have a fuse and rely on other protective devices in the circuit for overcurrent protection. Both types of disconnect switches serve the same purpose of isolating electrical equipment but offer different levels of protection.</p></p>
