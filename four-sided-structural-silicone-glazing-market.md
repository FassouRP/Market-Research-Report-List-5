<p><p>The four-sided structural silicone glazing market consists of various types such as aluminum structural framing, glass panels, silicone sealant, and EPDM. Aluminum structural framing involves using aluminum frames to support glass panels in a four-sided structural glazing system. Glass panels are the main component of the system, providing transparency and allowing light to enter buildings. Silicone sealant is used to bond the glass panels to the framing, providing a secure and weatherproof seal. EPDM is a type of rubber seal used to provide additional protection and insulation in the glazing system.</p></p>
