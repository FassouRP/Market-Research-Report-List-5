<p><p>Emergency care needleless connectors are medical devices used to connect IV tubing to an IV catheter or other medical devices without the need for a needle. The market is segmented into positive fluid displacement, negative fluid displacement, and neutral displacement connectors. Positive fluid displacement connectors have a mechanism that displaces fluid to ensure a closed system, while negative fluid displacement connectors have a mechanism that prevents fluid from leaking out during disconnection. Neutral displacement connectors maintain a neutral pressure within the system. Each type offers different benefits for patient care and infection control.</p></p>
