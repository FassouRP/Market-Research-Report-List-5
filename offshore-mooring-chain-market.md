<p><strong>The Offshore Mooring Chain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>StudLink</li><li>StudlessLink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offshore Mooring Chain Market includes two main types: StudLink and StudlessLink chains. StudLink chains have stud attachments on every link, providing high strength and reliability for offshore mooring applications. StudlessLink chains, on the other hand, do not have studs and offer flexibility and ease of handling due to their smooth surface. StudLink chains are preferred for heavy-duty operations requiring maximum strength, while StudlessLink chains are more commonly used in applications where ease of installation and displacement are key factors.</p></p>
