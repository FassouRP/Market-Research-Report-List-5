<p><ul><li>Systemic Insecticides</li><li>Contact Insecticides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agricultural insecticides are categorized into two main types: Systemic insecticides and contact insecticides. Systemic insecticides are absorbed by plants and provide long-lasting protection against insects that feed on them. Contact insecticides act upon direct contact with insects, killing them on contact. Both types of insecticides play a crucial role in protecting crops from insect damage, however, they vary in their mode of action and effectiveness, depending on the specific pest and crop being treated.</p></p>
