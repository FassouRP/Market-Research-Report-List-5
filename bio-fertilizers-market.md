<p><p>Bio fertilizers are classified into three main types based on their functions - Nitrogen-Fixing, Phosphate-Solubilizing, and Potash-Mobilizing. Nitrogen-Fixing bio fertilizers contain bacteria that convert atmospheric nitrogen into a form that plants can use. Phosphate-Solubilizing fertilizers contain microorganisms that help release phosphorus from insoluble compounds in the soil. Potash-Mobilizing bio fertilizers contain microorganisms that solubilize potassium for plant uptake. These bio fertilizers help enhance soil fertility and improve crop yield in a sustainable manner.</p></p>
