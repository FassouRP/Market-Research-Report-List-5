<p><p>Metal magnetic powder core market consists of various types, including MPP core, Sendust core, High Flux, and Fe-Si core. MPP core offers high saturation flux density and low core loss, making it ideal for high-frequency applications. Sendust core provides good magnetic permeability and low core loss, suitable for medium-frequency applications. High Flux core has high induction levels with low core loss, making it perfect for high-power applications. Fe-Si core offers high magnetic permeability and low core loss, making it suitable for low-frequency applications.</p></p>
