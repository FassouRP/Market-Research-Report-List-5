<p><ul><li>Continuous Coils</li><li>Bonnell Coils</li><li>Offset Coils</li><li>Marshall Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mattress spring wire market consists of various types such as Continuous Coils, Bonnell Coils, Offset Coils, and Marshall Coils. Continuous Coils are made from a single piece of wire that runs throughout the mattress, providing consistent support. Bonnell Coils are hourglass-shaped and connected by spiraled wire for durability. Offset Coils have flattened tops and bottoms for enhanced support and less noise. Marshall Coils, also known as pocket coils, are individually wrapped and offer better motion isolation. Each type caters to different preferences and needs in the mattress industry.</p></p>
