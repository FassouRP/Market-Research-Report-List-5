<p><p>Ultrafast Laser Drilling Machine Market consists of two main types: Single Pulse and Multipulse. Single Pulse drilling involves using a single, high-energy laser pulse to create a hole in the material. This type of drilling is typically used for precision drilling applications where a high level of accuracy is required. Multipulse drilling, on the other hand, involves using multiple lower energy pulses to drill holes. This method is often used for larger holes or when drilling through thicker materials that require more energy to penetrate.</p></p>
