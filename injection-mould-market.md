<p><p>In the injection mould market, there are various types including Two-plate Injection Mold, Three-plate Injection Mould, and Stack Injection Mold. Two-plate Injection Mold is a simple design with two plates that separate the part from the runner system. Three-plate Injection Mould has an additional plate that allows for an extra runner system, providing more complex part designs. Stack Injection Mold allows for multiple part cavities to be stacked vertically, increasing production efficiency and output. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the desired outcome of the injection moulding process.</p></p>
