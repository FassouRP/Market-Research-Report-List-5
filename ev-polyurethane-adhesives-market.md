<p><p>EV polyurethane adhesives are widely used in various industries because of their high strength, flexibility, and durability. The two main types of EV polyurethane adhesives are water-based and solvent-based. Water-based polyurethane adhesives are environmentally friendly and have low VOC emissions, making them suitable for applications where sustainability is a concern. On the other hand, solvent-based polyurethane adhesives offer higher initial bond strength and faster curing times, making them ideal for applications that require quick results.</p></p>
