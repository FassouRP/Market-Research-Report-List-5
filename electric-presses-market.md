<p><ul><li>Vertical Type Presses</li><li>Horizontal Type Presses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric presses are available in two main types: vertical type presses and horizontal type presses. Vertical type presses are designed to exert force vertically, making them ideal for applications where space is limited. They are commonly used for tasks such as assembling parts, pressing bearings, and bending metal sheets. Horizontal type presses, on the other hand, exert force horizontally and are typically used for tasks such as punching holes, straightening metal bars, and forming metal plates. Both types of presses come in various sizes and power capacities to suit different industrial needs.</p></p>
