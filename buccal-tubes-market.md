<p><p>Buccal tubes are orthodontic appliances used in braces to hold the archwire in place. There are two main types of buccal tubes in the market based on their design at the bottom: smooth bottom and net bottom. Smooth bottom buccal tubes have a flat surface at the bottom that minimizes friction with the archwire, allowing for smoother tooth movement. Net bottom buccal tubes have a perforated surface that allows for better bonding with the adhesive. Both types serve the same purpose of securing the archwire in place during orthodontic treatment.</p></p>
