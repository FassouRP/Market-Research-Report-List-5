<p><p>Tissue-based diagnostics market includes various types such as Immunohistochemistry (IHC), In Situ Hybridization (ISH), Digital Pathology and Workflow Management, and Special Staining. IHC involves using antibodies to identify specific proteins in tissue samples. ISH is used to detect specific RNA or DNA sequences within the tissue. Digital pathology allows for digital image analysis and management of pathology data. Special staining involves staining techniques to differentiate cellular components for better visualization under a microscope. These technologies are crucial in diagnosing and treating various diseases.</p></p>
