<p><p>Bipolar Disorders is a mental health condition characterized by extreme mood swings, including manic episodes and depressive episodes. Treatment options typically fall into five categories: Mood stabilizers, such as lithium, help regulate mood swings. Anticonvulsants, like valproate, can also stabilize mood. Antipsychotics, such as risperidone, are used to manage symptoms of psychosis. Antidepressants like SSRIs may be prescribed during depressive episodes. Antianxiety medications, such as benzodiazepines, can help manage anxiety symptoms associated with the disorder. These treatments aim to stabilize mood and manage symptoms of bipolar disorder.</p></p>
