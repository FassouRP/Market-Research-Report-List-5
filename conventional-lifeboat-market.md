<p><p>Conventional lifeboat market types include closed lifeboats and free-fall lifeboats. Closed lifeboats are enclosed vessels with a roof that protect occupants from harsh weather conditions and water. They are commonly used on larger ships and oil rigs. Free-fall lifeboats are designed to quickly launch into the water from a high position on a ship, allowing for rapid evacuation in emergency situations. Both types of lifeboats are essential for ensuring the safety of passengers and crew members during maritime emergencies.</p></p>
