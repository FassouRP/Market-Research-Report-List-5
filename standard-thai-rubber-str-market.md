TSR CV is concentrated latex with a low ammonia content, while TSR L is field latex with a higher ammonia content. TSR 5, TSR 10, and TSR 20 are various grades of technically specified rubber with different levels of physical properties. These different market types cater to various industries and applications, providing options for buyers based on their specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
