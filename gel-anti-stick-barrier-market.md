<p><p>Gel Anti-stick barrier market is categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable types. Non-absorbable barriers, on the other hand, remain in place and provide a longer-lasting protective barrier. Both types are used to prevent tissues from sticking together during surgical procedures, but the choice between absorbable and non-absorbable barriers depends on the specific needs of the patient and the nature of the surgery.</p></p>
