<p><p>Coronavirus Rapid Testing Kits come in various types depending on the sample used for testing - nose and throat swab, blood, sputum, and nasal aspirate. Nose and throat swab tests involve taking samples from the nasal cavity and throat to detect the virus. Blood tests analyze blood samples for antibodies or antigens. Sputum tests require a sample of mucus coughed up from the respiratory tract. Nasal aspirate tests involve collecting fluid from the nasal passages. Each type of test serves a specific purpose in detecting and diagnosing COVID-19.</p></p>
