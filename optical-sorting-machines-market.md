<p><strong>The Optical Sorting Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cameras</li><li>Lasers</li><li>NIR Sorters</li><li>Hyperspectral Cameras & Combined Sorters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical sorting machines are categorized based on the type of technology used for sorting. Camera-based sorters utilize high-resolution cameras to identify objects based on color, shape, and size. Laser sorters use laser technology to detect and separate materials based on their properties. Near Infrared (NIR) sorters analyze the molecular composition of objects for sorting. Hyperspectral cameras break down light into different wavelengths to identify unique characteristics of materials. Combined sorters integrate multiple technologies to achieve more precise and efficient sorting methods.</p></p>
